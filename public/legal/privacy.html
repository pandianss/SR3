Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 June 2026  ·  SuperRecall

SuperRecall ("we", "us", "our") operates the CAIIB Prep mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information. By signing in, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Google Account Data: Your name, email address, and profile photo via Google Sign-In. We do not access your Google password.
  • Study Progress: Spaced-repetition card states, session history, subject preferences, and exam readiness scores.
  • Onboarding Profile: Banking role, target elective, study hours, energy profile — used solely to personalise your study experience.
  • Purchase Data: Subscription and in-app purchase confirmations from Google Play. We never see your payment card details.
  • Referral Data: Referral code usage and relationships, to calculate commissions.
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous event data via Google Firebase Analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To sync your study progress across devices.
  • To personalise your study plan, subject ordering, and revision queue.
  • To send push notifications for due revision cards (you may disable in device settings).
  • To calculate and pay referral commissions.
  • To verify subscription status and unlock premium content.
  • To improve the App through anonymised analytics.
  • To respond to support requests.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore), encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. Firebase is ISO 27001 certified and SOC 2/3 compliant. Firestore security rules ensure only your account can access your records.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase — Authentication, database, analytics. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Billing — Subscription processing. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Gemini API — AI case studies and coaching, routed through our server. Your personal data is never sent to Gemini.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We share data only with Google services required to operate the App, when required by law, or in aggregate anonymised form for product improvement.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: Email us to export your study records.
  • Delete: Email us to permanently delete all data within 30 days.
  • Opt out of analytics: Disable in device app settings.
  • Withdraw consent: Sign out at any time.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for adult banking professionals (18+). We do not knowingly collect data from minors. Contact us immediately if you believe a minor has used the App.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of material changes via the App. Continued use after notification constitutes acceptance.
